# Privacy Policy — Hacker News Reader

Last updated: 2026-01-09

## Overview
Hacker News Reader is a Chrome extension that displays recent stories from Hacker News.

## Data Collection
Hacker News Reader does not collect, sell, or transfer personal data to the developer or to third parties.

## Data Usage
The extension:
- Fetches Hacker News RSS content from `https://news.ycombinator.com/rss` to display recent stories.
- Opens Hacker News links and a “Submit” link only when you click them.

## Local Storage
The extension stores data locally on your device using Chrome’s extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`) in order to:
- Save user preferences (refresh interval, whether to open links in background tabs)
- Cache recent feed items and the last refresh time

This data remains on your device and is not sent to the developer.

## Third Parties
The extension communicates only with:
- `news.ycombinator.com` (to fetch RSS and open Hacker News pages)

## Changes to This Policy
If this policy changes, it will be updated in this document.

## Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the publisher via the Chrome Web Store support channel for this extension.
